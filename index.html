Making an Emoji

The campaigns, dark money and fight for representation behind each emoji

Some of your favorite food emojis are the products of change.org petitions. Others were backed by corporate marketing campaigns. That Chinese takeout emoji? A major source of contention.

As emojis expanded from whimsical stand-ins to proof of representation—influence, even—in one of the most ubiquitous interfaces we use, the origin story behind every 😂 and 🎮 became of cultural import on its own.

Read the (not quite) thousand words behind each picture.
